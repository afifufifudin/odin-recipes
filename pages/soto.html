Soto Ayam: Fragrant Indonesian Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

For the Yellow Spice Paste:

  • 5 shallots, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, chopped
  • 1 inch turmeric, fresh or ground
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp white peppercorns
  • 1 candlenut (optional)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup water

For the broth:

  • 1 whole chicken (around 1.5kg)
  • 1.5L water
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 star anise
  • 3 lemongrass stalks, white parts only, bruised
  • 3 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (optional, for richer flavor)

For the Noodles and Toppings:

  • 200g rice vermicelli or yellow noodles
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, halved
  • 100g bean sprouts
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Fried shallots
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Sambal oelek (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Make the Spice Paste: Blend shallots, garlic, ginger, turmeric, spices, and candlenut (if using) into a smooth paste. Heat oil in a pan and fry the paste until fragrant and slightly darker. Add water and cook until thickened.
  2. Simmer the Broth: In a large pot, combine chicken, water, spices, salt, and the cooked spice paste. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour, or until chicken is cooked through. Remove chicken and let cool, then shred the meat.
  3. Assemble the Soup: Strain the broth and return it to the pot. Add coconut milk (if using) and bring back to a simmer. Add noodles and cook until tender.
  4. Serve: Divide noodles and broth among bowls. Top with shredded chicken, halved eggs, bean sprouts, lime wedges, fried shallots, and cilantro. Drizzle with sambal oelek (optional) for a spicy kick.

Tips

      You can substitute chicken thighs for a richer flavor.
      Feel free to add other vegetables like carrots, cabbage, or celery to the broth.
      Leftover soto ayam tastes even better the next day! Store it in the fridge for up to 3 days.

Embrace the comforting warmth of soto ayam, a dish that speaks of Indonesian culinary heritage. With its fragrant broth, tender chicken, and vibrant toppings, it's a guaranteed way to nourish your body and soul.
